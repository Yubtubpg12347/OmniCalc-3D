The Physics Behind OmniCalc

1. Solid State Volume & Mass: Volume ($V$) is calculated using exact mathematical coordinate integrations for each unique surface mesh. Using the density ($\rho$) of selected atoms: Mass (M) = V × ρ

2. Force Vectoring: Weight Force ($F_{gravity}$) represents gravity acting downwards from the body's center of mass. Added to our manually loaded forces: F_total = (Mass × g) + F_applied

3. Snells Refraction Index: If photon rays hit a transparent barrier, their transmission velocity and direction shifts proportional to Snell's Law ($n_1 \sin \theta_1 = n_2 \sin \theta_2$).

4. Mechanical Ground Stress: Normal Force representing standard static reaction forces pushes upwards directly opposing all gravitational and load stresses.
